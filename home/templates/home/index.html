WHturk is a website for crowdsourced mapping of Wormhole system connections in EvE Online.

The site is currently in testing. Due to the CREST API not giving accurate character location updates, the tracker will not always update your location.

While the site is under construction, users will be given free points to test on the map page.

How it works

Scout

The scout tracker will follow your location in space. As you discover new wormhole connections, you will be awarded points based on how many other people have mapped that connection. Discover new wormholes for maximum points!

Map

The WHturk Map draws a dynamic layout of the connections between known space and wormhole systems. You can search for the shortest route between any two systems - including routes through known wormhole space. When searching for a route through wormhole space, the wormhole systems will be masked by default. If you have enough points, you can purchase the route and unmask the wormhole systems.

Turk for ISK

In the future, WHturk points will be tradable for ingame ISK. Suppliment your wormhole exploration earnings by cashing out your scouting points, or purchase points with ISK to find that surprise backdoor for your fleet.
